Go-to stir-fry sauce

The saviour of any busy weeknight.

Ingredients

  • 500g tofu, cut into strips
  • 3 tbsp light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp honey or brown sugar/li>
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp chili powder or 1 tbsp sriracha (to taste)
  • 1 tsp Chinese five-spice powder
  • 1 carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 2 spring onions, green parts only

Steps

  1. Mix everything together except the carrots and spring onions in a bowl and chill, covered, in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp vegetable oil in a wok on high heat.
  3. Add the carrots and beef/tofu, reserving the remaining marinade, and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the remaining marinade and cook for a further minute until the sauce is reduced. If it gets too thick, add a splash of water.
  5. Serve over rice or noodles and add the spring onions on top. Drizzle on some more sesame oil and a bit of srirarcha/hot sauce if you like.
